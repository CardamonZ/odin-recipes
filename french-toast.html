Fluffy French Toast Recipe

Description

This is the best French toast recipe. It's different because it uses flour.
I have given it to some friends and they've all liked it better than the French toast they usually make!


Ingredients:

  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 12 thick slices bread

Directions

    Step 1
  1. Measure flour into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in the milk. Whisk in eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt until smooth.
    2. Step 2
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
    3. Step 3
  3. Soak bread slices in milk mixture until saturated.
    4. Step 4
  4. Working in batches, cook bread on the preheated griddle or pan until golden brown on each side. Serve hot.
